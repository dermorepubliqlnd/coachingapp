1 Where is this app hosted?

Upload coaching-app.html to a SharePoint document library (e.g. your L&D Apps library). Both users open it from that SharePoint URL. Your existing Microsoft login handles all data access — no Azure app registration needed.

💡 If you open this file locally (not from SharePoint), the app works in offline mode — data saves to your browser only and won't sync with the supervisor.
